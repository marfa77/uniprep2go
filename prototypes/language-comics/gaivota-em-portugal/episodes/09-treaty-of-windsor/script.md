# Ep.09 — Treaty of Windsor · Script

Complete comic story. Artistic interpretation of Windsor, 1386.
Human-scale: court corridors, letters, ceremony — no gore, no battle scenes.

**Through-line:** Beatriz (Portuguese court attendant) + Tom (young English page at Windsor).  
**Witness:** Gaivota.  
**Distant presence:** ambassadors / kings — seen, barely spoken to.

---

## Comic Page 1 — Setup

**Visual:** Dawn at Windsor Castle — mist on the Thames. Beatriz carries a sealed letter with the Portuguese arms. Tom holds a torch in a stone corridor. Gaivota on a crenellated wall.

**Gaivota:** Dois reinos… uma manhã fria.

**Beatriz:** A carta chegou da corte de Lisboa.

**Tom:** My lord waits. The ink is still wet.

---

## Comic Page 2 — Rising action

**Visual:** Great hall preparations — banners, interpreters, long table. Beatriz arranges wax seals; Tom sets out cups. Gaivota wheeling above the standard.

**Beatriz:** Tradução cuidada. Cada palavra… pesa.

**Tom:** Peace is heavier than a sword today.

**Gaivota:** O vento traz promessas através do canal.

---

## Comic Page 3 — Turning point

**Visual:** Signing moment — hands on parchment, not combat. Ambassadors stand; Beatriz and Tom at the edge, witnesses. Gaivota above the treaty scroll.

**Embaixador:** Pelo rei de Portugal. Pelo rei de Inglaterra.

**Beatriz:** Assinado. A aliança nasce.

**Gaivota:** Um tratado… mais forte que muralhas.

---

## Comic Page 4 — Consequence

**Visual:** Courtyard celebration — modest, dignified. Beatriz and Tom share bread on a step. Horses ready for the return journey. Gaivota on a pennant pole.

**Tom:** Will it last?

**Beatriz:** Durará. Porque ambos precisam.

**Gaivota:** Amizade antiga… ainda sem rugas.

---

## Comic Page 5 — Resolution

**Visual:** Sunset over Windsor. Beatriz mounts a horse with the sealed copy. Tom waves from the gate. Gaivota lifts toward the west.

**Beatriz:** Levo isto a Lisboa.

**Tom:** And I will remember this day.

**Gaivota:** Guardo este Windsor. Portugal e Inglaterra… de mãos dadas.

*(End. No "to be continued.")*
