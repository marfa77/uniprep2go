## History brief

**Hook:** After a brutal ocean voyage, Portuguese ships reached Calicut in 1498 — Europe and India met by sea.

**Bullets:**
- Date: **May 1498** (arrival at Calicut / Kozhikode)
- Place: **Malabar Coast**, India — after rounding Africa
- Who: **Vasco da Gama** and crew; local rulers and merchants at Calicut
- How: Atlantic and Indian Ocean navigation; monsoon winds; guides along East Africa
- After: a sea route to Asian spices — trade, contact, and a new global age
