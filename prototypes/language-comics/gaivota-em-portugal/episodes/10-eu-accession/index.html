Portugal joins the EEC

On 1 January 1986 Portugal became a member of the European Communities — a modern hinge after dictatorship and democracy’s return.

  • When: 1 January 1986
  • Where: Portugal (and Europe)
  • Who: Portuguese citizens after the Carnation Revolution path
  • How: accession to the EEC / European Communities
  • After: funds, mobility, and a new European chapter

A father and daughter watch the country join a wider neighbourhood — flags, news, and the quiet pride of an open window.

The comic that follows is an artistic interpretation. Human-scale family story; hopeful modern tone.

Cultural note

On 1 January 1986 Portugal joined the European Communities (EEC), a landmark after the return to democracy. The comic follows a family morning — flags, news, and the sense that Portugal had opened a window without leaving itself behind.
